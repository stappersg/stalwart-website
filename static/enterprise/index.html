Enterprise edition

Elevate your email platform to enterprise level.

Stalwart Enterprise Edition is designed to elevate your email infrastructure to new heights, surpassing the robust foundation of our open-source edition. This premium version offers a suite of additional features tailored to meet the demands of enterprise environments, ensuring superior flexibility, security, and customization. With a commercial license, you can enjoy the full power of Stalwart without the limitations of the AGPLv3 license, giving you the freedom to customize and integrate our solution seamlessly into your existing infrastructure. Moreover, the Enterprise Edition includes priority support and expedited bug fixing, ensuring that your email system remains reliable and efficient. Invest in Stalwart Enterprise Edition to elevate your email infrastructure with professional-grade tools and dedicated support, driving your business forward with confidence and ease.

Metrics & reporting

Real-time insights and alerts for complete email oversight.

The Enterprise version of Stalwart includes a sophisticated dashboard designed to provide comprehensive visibility into your email server's performance and health. This feature-rich dashboard allows you to visualize a wide array of metrics, giving you real-time insights into server operations, email traffic, and system performance. With the ability to generate detailed reports, you can easily track trends, identify issues, and ensure optimal performance. Furthermore, the dashboard seamlessly integrates with monitoring systems like Prometheus, enabling you to leverage existing infrastructure for centralized monitoring and alerting. In the event of any issues, the system will automatically send alerts to administrators, ensuring prompt attention and resolution. This proactive approach helps maintain system reliability and minimizes downtime, allowing your business to operate smoothly and efficiently.

Multi-tenancy

Streamlined management with multi-tenant support.

Multi-tenancy is a powerful feature that enables a single email server to support multiple organizations or tenants, each with its own isolated domain and user management capabilities. In the Enterprise version of Stalwart, the multi-tenancy feature allows you to delegate administrative powers to specific accounts, empowering them to manage their own company's domains, accounts, and settings independently. This not only enhances operational efficiency but also ensures that each tenant maintains control over their environment. With this robust feature, Stalwart Enterprise Edition provides the scalability and flexibility needed to accommodate growing businesses and diverse organizational structures, all while maintaining high levels of security and performance.

Branding

Tailored branding for a cohesive experience.

The Enterprise version of Stalwart offers comprehensive branding support, allowing you to tailor the web-based admin and service portal to reflect your company's unique identity. With the ability to replace all logos and incorporate your company name in place of the product name, you can ensure a consistent brand experience for your users. This customization not only enhances brand recognition but also fosters a sense of ownership and professionalism. By aligning the email management interface with your corporate branding, you can create a cohesive and branded experience that reinforces your organization's image and values at every interaction.

AI-powered

Harness the power of AI for superior email security.

Stalwart Enterprise leverages AI technology to provide unparalleled email security and management. With AI-powered features, Stalwart Enterprise excels in accurately classifying spam, detecting sophisticated phishing attempts, and blocking various types of network attacks. This intelligent approach ensures that your email environment remains secure and reliable. Stalwart Enterprise comes equipped with a pre-trained large language model (LLM), offering robust out-of-the-box protection. Additionally, it supports integration with leading AI providers such as OpenAI, Anthropic, and other cutting-edge platforms, allowing you to enhance and customize your security measures. By utilizing AI, Stalwart Enterprise delivers a smarter, more efficient email solution that proactively safeguards your communications and data.

Enterprise features

Comprehensive features for enhanced security and compliance.

Stalwart Enterprise offers a range of additional features designed to enhance security, compliance, and flexibility. These include expanded authentication options such as third-party OIDC providers, and SAML, providing robust and versatile ways to secure user access. Furthermore, Stalwart Enterprise supports lawful interception, a capability that allows authorized entities to monitor and access email communications for legal and regulatory purposes. This feature ensures that your organization can comply with legal requirements while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security. Together, these enhancements make Stalwart Enterprise a comprehensive and adaptable solution that meets the diverse needs of modern enterprises.

Priority support (*)

Expert assistance for enterprise needs.

Stalwart Enterprise provides your organization with comprehensive professional support, ensuring that your needs are met with the highest priority. This ensures that your team can quickly resolve any issues with expert guidance and dedicated resources. Additionally, Stalwart Enterprise customers benefit from priority bug fixing, ensuring that critical issues are addressed promptly to maintain seamless operation. Moreover, subscribing to the Enterprise version provides your organization with some influence over the product's roadmap, allowing your feedback and needs to shape future developments. This level of support and engagement ensures that Stalwart Enterprise is not only a powerful email and collaboration solution but also a responsive and evolving platform tailored to your business requirements.

(*) Priority support and bug-fixing are only available for deployments with 150 or more mailboxes.

Custom SLA

Tailored SLA for your needs.

A Service Level Agreement (SLA) is a formal commitment between a service provider and a client, defining the expected level of service, performance standards, and responsibilities. With Stalwart Enterprise, you can establish a custom SLA tailored to your organization's unique requirements. This ensures that you receive the precise level of support, response times, and performance guarantees necessary to maintain your email infrastructure at peak efficiency. By agreeing on a custom SLA, you can align Stalwart's services with your business objectives, ensuring a reliable and responsive email solution that meets your specific operational needs.
