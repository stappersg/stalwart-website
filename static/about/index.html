About the company

Stalwart Labs combines decades of expertise with modern technology to redefine the email server landscape.

Stalwart Labs, founded in July 2019, is committed to transforming the email server industry. The development of our flagship product, Stalwart Mail Server, began in November 2021, with the first release launched in September 2022. Although still a relatively new product, it is built on a foundation of over three decades of expertise in the field.

The company was initiated by a developer with more than 30 years of experience in creating email and enterprise software, who recognized the stagnation in the email server market. With most products in the 2020s still relying on outdated technology and cumbersome configuration processes, Stalwart Labs set out to create a modern, all-in-one mail server solution.

Stalwart Labs is growing rapidly. While the project initially started with one developer, the company is now expanding its team, actively hiring backend and frontend developers, support staff, and specialized sales personnel to better serve our expanding customer base.

With a presence in both the UK and the US, Stalwart Labs is dedicated to delivering innovative, secure, and easy-to-maintain email server solutions. Our mission is to drive the future of email technology, and we are excited to continue evolving to meet the needs of our customers in this fast-changing landscape.
